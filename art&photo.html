Photography

1. This is an individual event conducted nationally and online.
2. Each participant is allowed to submit only one entry.
3. Theme : Sur-Realism.
4. Photos must be submitted in JPEG format.
5. Photos containing offensive content or content unrelated to the theme will be disqualified.
6. Plagiarism is strictly prohibited, and entries must be the original work of the participant.
7. Submissions will be evaluated based on Adherence to the theme, Creativity and originality of the photo.
8. Participants must submit their entries to the provided email address: strangersculturals@gmail.com.
9. Ensure that submissions are made before the last submission date, as late entries will not be accepted.
10. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.

Product Photography

1. This is an individual event conducted nationally and hosted online.
2. Participants are required to photograph a product in a creative and professional manner.
3. The product to be photographed can be chosen by the participant but must align with the designated theme provided for the event.
4. Participants may submit only one entry.
5. Entries must be in JPEG format with high resolution and must not exceed the specified size 20MB.
6. Submissions containing offensive, inappropriate, or irrelevant content will be disqualified.
7. The use of copyrighted images or pre-existing work is strictly prohibited. Submissions must be the original work of the participant.
8. All entries must be submitted to the official event email: strangersculturals@gmail.com.
9. The submission deadline must be strictly adhered to; late submissions will not be accepted.
10. Submissions will be evaluated based on Creativity and innovation in presenting the product. Lighting, composition, and technical quality of the photograph. Relevance to the theme. Overall visual impact and storytelling ability.
11. The photograph must focus on highlighting the product effectively.
12. Use of props or backdrops is permitted but should not overshadow the product.
13. The judges’ decisions will be final and binding.
14. Any violation of the rules, including plagiarism, will result in disqualification.
